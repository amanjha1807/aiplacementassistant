# CRED — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
CRED is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: CRED, product-based, fintech, India.

## Hiring Process
Coding round followed by technical interviews with high emphasis on code quality and product craftsmanship, plus a culture round.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Technical interviews (DSA + system design). 3) Culture/product-sense round.

## 2026 Updates
Interviewers increasingly probe product taste alongside technical skill, given CRED's design-forward brand.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate was asked not just to solve a DSA problem but to discuss how they'd expose the result to end users in a clean, trustworthy way.

## Preparation Tips
Be ready to connect technical decisions to user-facing quality and trust, not just correctness — CRED interviews reward this framing.
