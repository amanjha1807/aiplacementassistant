# Zoho — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Zoho is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Zoho, product-based, SaaS, India.

## Hiring Process
Zoho uses its own in-house written test (the Zoho aptitude test), notably harder and more idiosyncratic than typical service-company tests, with strong emphasis on logical/output-prediction questions, followed by technical and HR interviews.

## Interview Rounds
1) Zoho written test (logical reasoning, output-prediction, coding). 2) Technical interview (DSA + LLD). 3) HR round.

## 2026 Updates
The written test's difficulty and idiosyncrasy have remained a key differentiator; no major structural change reported for 2026.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate described several output-prediction questions requiring careful manual tracing of code, distinct from typical algorithmic problems.

## Preparation Tips
Specifically practice code output-prediction questions (not just problem-solving) — Zoho's written test style differs meaningfully from other companies'.
