# Apple — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Apple is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Apple, product-based, big tech.

## Hiring Process
Team-specific and less standardized than other Tier 1 companies; expect deep technical drill-down from the specific team plus a strong focus on product taste and attention to detail.

## Interview Rounds
1) Recruiter screen. 2) Team-specific technical interviews (varies significantly by team). 3) Behavioral/culture interview focused on craftsmanship and detail orientation.

## 2026 Updates
No major structural change, but interviewers increasingly probe how candidates evaluate AI-generated design or code suggestions for quality and polish.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate described being asked to critique a piece of AI-generated UI code for subtle quality and accessibility issues.

## Preparation Tips
Research the specific team you're interviewing with as deeply as possible — Apple's process rewards team-specific depth over generic breadth.
