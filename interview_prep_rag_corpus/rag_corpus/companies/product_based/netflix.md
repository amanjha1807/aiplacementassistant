# Netflix — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Netflix is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Netflix, product-based, streaming.

## Hiring Process
Hiring skews senior-weighted; interviews focus heavily on real-world system tradeoffs and culture fit rather than pure algorithmic puzzles.

## Interview Rounds
1) Recruiter screen. 2) Technical interview(s) focused on system tradeoffs relevant to the role. 3) Culture interview centered on "freedom and responsibility."

## 2026 Updates
No major structural change; AI-driven recommendation and content-pipeline questions appear more often for relevant teams.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate for a backend role was asked to reason through the tradeoffs of a caching strategy for a global video-streaming platform rather than solve an abstract algorithm problem.

## Preparation Tips
Prepare real-world tradeoff discussions (latency, cost, reliability) rather than pure LeetCode-style prep; be ready to defend opinions confidently, which fits Netflix's culture.
