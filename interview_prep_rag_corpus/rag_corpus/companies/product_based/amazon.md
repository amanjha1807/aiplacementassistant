# Amazon — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Amazon is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Amazon, product-based, big tech, Leadership Principles.

## Hiring Process
Online Assessment (DSA + work-style survey) followed by phone screens and an onsite loop including a Bar Raiser round, all interviews mapped explicitly to Amazon's Leadership Principles.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Phone screen. 3) Onsite loop: multiple DSA rounds, one system design round (senior levels), and a Bar Raiser round.

## 2026 Updates
Behavioral answers are now explicitly mapped to Leadership Principles including a newer one emphasizing "Deliver Results with AI-assisted efficiency," and interviewers commonly follow up asking how the candidate used AI tools responsibly on a real project.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate answering an 'Ownership' behavioral question was asked a direct follow-up: 'Did you use any AI tools while solving that problem, and how did you verify the output was correct?'

## Preparation Tips
Map every STAR story explicitly to a Leadership Principle before the interview. Prepare at least one story that touches on responsible AI-tool usage.
