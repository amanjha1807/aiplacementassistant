# Swiggy — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Swiggy is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Swiggy, product-based, food delivery, India.

## Hiring Process
Online coding round followed by technical interviews mixing DSA (especially graph/geo problems) and system design, plus a culture round.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Technical interviews (DSA + system design). 3) Culture/hiring-manager round.

## 2026 Updates
System design prompts increasingly focus on real-time logistics scale, e.g., "design for surge demand during a cricket-match evening."

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate was asked to design a delivery-partner matching system optimizing for both distance and estimated delivery time.

## Preparation Tips
Practice graph algorithms (Dijkstra, BFS/DFS on grids) specifically — logistics companies over-index on these relative to generic DSA lists.
