# Meta — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Meta is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Meta, product-based, big tech.

Online coding assessment followed by phone screens and an onsite loop of two DSA rounds, one system design round, and one behavioral round based on Meta's values.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Phone screen. 3) Onsite: two DSA rounds, one system design round, one behavioral round.

Coding bar remains high on graph and dynamic programming problems; behavioral rounds continue to weight Meta's core values heavily.

A candidate reported two back-to-back DSA rounds heavily weighted toward graph traversal and DP, followed by a system design round on a news-feed-style product.
