# Salesforce — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Salesforce is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Salesforce, product-based, SaaS, enterprise.

## Hiring Process
Coding round (often on HackerRank/CodeSignal) followed by a technical interview with heavy LLD/design-pattern focus, plus a values/culture interview.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Technical interview (DSA + LLD/design patterns). 3) Values/culture round.

## 2026 Updates
Candidates are increasingly asked to critique or extend AI-generated code snippets as part of the technical round, reflecting internal Copilot-style tooling adoption.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate was shown an AI-generated class implementation and asked to identify a SOLID-principle violation and refactor it.

## Preparation Tips
Brush up on SOLID principles and 3–4 common design patterns (Factory, Singleton, Observer, Strategy) — these show up disproportionately at Salesforce.
