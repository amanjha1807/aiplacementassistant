# Flipkart — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Flipkart is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Flipkart, product-based, e-commerce, India.

## Hiring Process
Online coding round (2 problems, medium difficulty) followed by 2–3 technical interviews mixing DSA and system design, and a hiring-manager/culture round.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Technical interviews (DSA + system design). 3) Hiring-manager/culture round.

## 2026 Updates
Scale-focused system design questions are common, e.g., "design for 10 million concurrent orders during a flash sale," given Flipkart's Big Billion Days-scale events.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate's system design round centered on inventory consistency and checkout concurrency during a simulated flash-sale scenario.

## Preparation Tips
Know basic distributed-systems vocabulary (idempotency, eventual consistency, rate limiting, message queues) — it comes up even at SDE-1 level.
