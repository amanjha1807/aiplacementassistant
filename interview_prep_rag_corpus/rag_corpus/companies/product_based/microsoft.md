# Microsoft — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Microsoft is a Product based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Microsoft, product-based, big tech.

## Hiring Process
Online coding assessment followed by phone/video screens and an onsite loop covering DSA, system design (senior roles), and behavioral rounds tied to Microsoft's culture.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online Assessment. 2) Technical phone screen. 3) Onsite loop: DSA, system design, and a behavioral/culture round.

## 2026 Updates
System design rounds for 2026 frequently include an AI-feature integration angle, e.g., "design a feature that adds an AI assistant to this product."

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate's system design round asked them to design a document-editing product and then extend it with an AI writing-assistant feature, discussing latency and cost trade-offs for the AI calls.

## Preparation Tips
Prepare to extend a classic system design answer with an AI-feature layer — think about where an LLM call fits, its latency budget, and fallback behavior if it fails.
