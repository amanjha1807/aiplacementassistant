Wipro hires through the NLTH/Elite pipeline: an online test with Aptitude, Verbal, Coding, and an Essay-writing section.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online test (aptitude, verbal, coding, essay). 2) Combined Technical + HR panel round, often merged into a single interview for efficiency.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate described being asked to write a 150-word essay on "AI and the future of work," followed by a panel interview blending technical basics with behavioral questions.

## Preparation Tips
