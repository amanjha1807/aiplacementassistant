# Cognizant — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Cognizant is a Service based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Cognizant, service-based, IT services, GenC.

## Hiring Process
Cognizant hires via GenC, GenC Next, and GenC Elevate tracks with tiered difficulty. The online assessment includes aptitude, coding, and increasingly an asynchronous, AI-scored communication video round.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online assessment (aptitude, coding, async video). 2) Technical interview with core CS fundamentals and one DSA problem. 3) Conversational HR round.

## 2026 Updates
The asynchronous video communication round is now common across GenC drives and is scored algorithmically on clarity, tone, and structure alongside content.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate described recording answers to three prompts via webcam with no interviewer present, followed later by a live technical round only after passing the video screen.

## Preparation Tips
Practice speaking clearly on camera for the asynchronous video round — tone and pacing are scored alongside content, so rehearse out loud, not just mentally.
