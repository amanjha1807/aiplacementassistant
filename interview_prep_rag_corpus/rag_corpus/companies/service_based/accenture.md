# Accenture — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Accenture is a Service based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Accenture, service-based, IT consulting, Digital, Cloud, AI.

## Hiring Process
Accenture uses a Cognitive + Technical assessment (verbal, logical, coding), followed by a Technical interview and an HR round.

## Interview Rounds
1) Cognitive/technical online assessment. 2) Technical interview covering programming basics, a DSA problem, and cloud/AI-service familiarity for Digital/Cloud/AI-focused hires. 3) HR round evaluating cultural fit and client-facing readiness.

## 2026 Updates
A growing share of Accenture roles are tied to GenAI delivery teams, so interviewers frequently ask candidates to explain a recent AI concept (RAG, fine-tuning, prompt engineering) in plain language, even for non-AI roles.

## Sample Candidate Experience
One candidate for a non-AI role was still asked to explain, in under two minutes, what Retrieval-Augmented Generation is and why companies use it.

## Preparation Tips
Have a simple, accurate one-paragraph explanation of RAG, LLMs, and prompt engineering ready, regardless of your target role.
