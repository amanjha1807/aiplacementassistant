# HCLTech — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
HCLTech is a Service based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: HCLTech, service-based, IT services, TechBee, Fresher Engineer Trainee.

## Hiring Process
HCLTech hires through TechBee (early-career/diploma-friendly) and Fresher Engineer Trainee programs, alongside standard lateral hiring, with an online aptitude + coding test followed by interviews.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online aptitude + coding test. 2) Technical interview on fundamentals (DBMS, OOP, networking basics) and one or two DSA problems. 3) HR round.

## 2026 Updates
HCLTech's entry-level programs continue to emphasize trainability and communication over raw coding difficulty, while enterprise-AI delivery roles now include GenAI-literacy questions.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A TechBee applicant reported a technical round focused mostly on basic networking concepts and willingness-to-learn questions rather than hard DSA problems.

## Preparation Tips
Prepare 2–3 STAR-format stories about teamwork and adaptability — HCLTech weights cultural fit and trainability heavily relative to coding difficulty at entry level.
