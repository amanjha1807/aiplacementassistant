# Infosys — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Infosys is a Service based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Infosys, service-based, IT services, InfyTQ, Specialist Programmer.

## Hiring Process
Infosys hires via the InfyTQ / Infosys assessment, split between Infosys SP (Specialist Programmer) for stronger coders and standard Digital/Power Programmer tracks. The test covers Aptitude, Pseudocode reading, and Coding.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online assessment (aptitude, pseudocode, coding). 2) Technical interview covering core CS fundamentals plus one easy-medium DSA problem. 3) HR round assessing adaptability and long-term commitment.

## 2026 Updates
Infosys has expanded Specialist Programmer intake and added a lightweight system-design question (e.g., "design a simple URL shortener") even at fresher level for SP applicants.

## Sample Candidate Experience
Candidates frequently report pseudocode-reading questions that are unlike typical LeetCode-style problems, requiring careful line-by-line trace rather than pattern recognition.

## Preparation Tips
Practice pseudocode-reading questions specifically since they appear almost every hiring cycle. Revise time and space complexity analysis explicitly, since interviewers probe reasoning over memorized answers.
