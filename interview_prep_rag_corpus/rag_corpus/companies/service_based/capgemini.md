# Capgemini — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
Capgemini is a Service based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: Capgemini, service-based, IT consulting.

## Hiring Process
Capgemini's process includes an online aptitude + coding test followed by a technical interview that leans toward scenario-based, client-facing questions.

## Interview Rounds
1) Online test. 2) Technical interview with scenario-based questions ("a client asks for X, how do you respond?") alongside core fundamentals. 3) HR round.

## 2026 Updates
Consulting-style scenario questions increasingly include a light AI-adoption angle, e.g., "a client wants to add an AI chatbot — what would you ask them before starting?"

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate described a technical round that spent as much time on a client-communication scenario as on an actual coding problem.

## Preparation Tips
Practice structuring scenario-based answers clearly: clarify the client's real need first, then propose a solution, then mention trade-offs.
