# TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) — Interview Experience & Hiring Guide (2026)

## Overview
TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) is a Service based company. This file documents its 2026 hiring pipeline, interview rounds, a representative candidate experience, and preparation tips. Metadata tags: TCS, service-based, IT services, NQT.

## Hiring Process
TCS hires primarily through the TCS NQT (National Qualifier Test), which covers Numerical Ability, Verbal Ability, Reasoning Ability, and Programming Logic. High performers can qualify for TCS Digital (higher package, tougher bar) versus TCS Ninja (standard track).

## Interview Rounds
1) Online NQT test with an AI-assisted problem-solving segment. 2) Technical interview covering OOP, DBMS, OS fundamentals, and one live coding problem. 3) HR/Managerial round assessing communication and flexibility for relocation and shift work.

## 2026 Updates
The NQT now includes a short segment where candidates are shown AI-generated code containing a bug and must identify and fix it. Domain-specific tracks (Cloud, AI/ML, Cybersecurity) have their own technical rounds with domain questions layered on top of core CS fundamentals.

## Sample Candidate Experience
A candidate reported the technical interview opened with "walk me through a project on your resume," moved into SQL joins and normalization questions, and closed with a live problem on array rotation, where the interviewer cared more about the approach narration than exact syntax.

## Preparation Tips
Be fluent end-to-end in one language (Java, Python, or C++). Know DBMS normal forms and OS process scheduling cold. Prepare a 90-second project walkthrough you can deliver smoothly.
